SIDEKICKSPACE

This website helps you mask important information and replace it with randomly generated strings before sending it to AI. You can easily replace the random strings with the original information from the AI output.

Step 1: Enter your information and highlight words in a left-to-right sequence. The highlighted words will be shown in the button below.

Step 2: After highlighting the words, click the button below. The highlighted words will be automatically replaced in the input above, and you can then copy the modified text to use with AI.

Step 3: Copy the AI-generated output and click the Replace button to substitute the random strings with the original information. Finally, you can copy the modified output by clicking the Copy button.

Click the Refresh Page button to reset the process, clearing all information and starting afresh.

Are you sure you want to refresh the page? All information will be cleared.
