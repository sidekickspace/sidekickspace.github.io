SIDEKICKSPACE

Welcome to our website! Here, we've got a cool feature that helps you keep your important information safe and private. We'll mask it with some cleverly generated random strings before sending it off to the AI. And the best part? You can easily swap those random strings back to the original information with just a click, thanks to our smart AI output. So, no worries, your data stays protected and under your control!

Text has been copied!

Step 1: Type in your information and hover over the important words to highlight them. Those words will show up in a button right in the middle of the input box. When you click the button, the selected words will replace the text in the input field. Or if you prefer, you can click the "Select" button. All the words you typed will be shown below the input box, and you can pick the specific words you want. The selected words will be added to the label of a button displayed right in the center of the screen. Give it a click, and watch those words instantly replace the text in the input field.

Step 2: Take a moment to review the key-value pairs and make sure everything looks good. If you spot any mistakes, no worries! You can simply click the delete button, and all the keys will be replaced with the original text once again.

Step 3: Grab the AI-generated output and give it a good ol' copy. When you're ready, hit that Replace button to swap out those random strings with the original information. You can then easily copy the modified output by simply clicking the Copy button.

Click the Refresh Page button to reset the process, clearing all information and starting afresh.
