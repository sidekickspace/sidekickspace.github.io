Welcome to our innovative text privacy tool – a solution designed to safeguard your sensitive information while harnessing the power of generative AI. With privacy concerns growing in the digital age, we've developed a unique way to protect your data without compromising on the capabilities of cutting-edge AI technologies.

Our tool operates on a simple yet effective principle. We understand that sharing sensitive information with generative AI can be worrisome. That's why we've created a seamless process that allows you to interact with AI while keeping your data confidential.

How does it work? Our tool replaces sensitive words and phrases with random strings, making sure that the original information remains hidden. You can then paste this modified text into the generative AI input box, confident that your private details are shielded from direct exposure.

Once you receive the AI-generated output, our tool lets you effortlessly revert the text to its original form. No more compromising between utilizing advanced AI capabilities and protecting your privacy – now you can have both.

Experience the freedom to explore generative AI without the fear of sharing confidential data. Give our text privacy tool a try and take control of your information like never before.

An Example Use Case

Let's explore a typical scenario: Imagine you're crafting a job application cover letter. To leverage AI's help without revealing personal details, you input your cover letter into our tool. It's then transformed into a secure version with random strings. After AI processing, you easily restore the original content, gaining valuable insights while maintaining privacy.

Step 1: Input or Paste Your Text

Start by typing or pasting the text that you want to send to the generative AI into the input box.

After inputting your text, click the right arrow button circled in the screenshot image to proceed.

Step 2: Protect with Randomization

Now that you've entered your text, it's time to protect it using random strings. Click the word buttons shown in the screenshot image. Each button click will replace specific words in the text with random strings.

Your text will be transformed into a secure version with random strings replacing sensitive information.

Step 3: An Alternate Approach to Text Replacement

There's an alternative method for replacing text. Highlight the text you want to protect in the input box. Click the 'Replace' button to replace the selected words with random strings.

Once all sensitive information is replaced, click the 'Copy and Go' button (circled in the screenshot image) to proceed to the next step.

Step 4: Generate AI Output

Your modified text is now ready to be used with the generative AI. Paste the transformed text into the AI's input box.

Once the AI generates the output, copy the result to continue.

Step 5: Restore Original Text

Now that you have the AI-generated output, navigate back to the original webpage where you initially used the tool.

Paste the AI-generated text into the designated input box. Then, click the 'Restore & Copy' button (circled in the screenshot image) to replace the random strings with the original words.

Your text is now restored to its original form, with sensitive information back in place.

Step 6: Complete the Process

Your restored text is now ready to be copied and used. All original words have been reinstated.

If you'd like to start over with different text, you have options. You can click the 'Start Over' button to reset the tool, or simply refresh the webpage to initiate the process anew.

Feel free to explore and utilize the tool to protect your sensitive information whenever you need.
